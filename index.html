Clean burn, generous throw.

We pour in small batches to guarantee consistent fragrance throw from the first light to the last flicker. Our coconut-soy blend delivers up to 60 hours of burn time per 13 oz vessel.

Crafted to be repurposed.

Every glass tumbler is designed for a second life. Once your candle is finished, rinse with warm water to transform it into a bud vase, pen cup, or cocktail tumbler.

From Brooklyn with love.

Celestial Candle Studio was born in a sunlit loft overlooking the East River. We translate travel memories into olfactive stories using sustainable wax, phthalate-free oils, and vessels you will treasure long after the burn.

  • Female-founded and independently owned since 2016.
  • Small batch pouring ensures uncompromising quality and freshness.
  • We donate 2% of every purchase to environmental restoration projects.
Studio Notes Batch No. 342

Sustainably minded from wick to delivery.

Plant-based wax

Our custom coconut-soy blend is renewable, biodegradable, and delivers the cleanest burn we have ever tested.

Non-toxic fragrance

Perfume-grade oils are free from phthalates, parabens, and animal byproducts—safe for you, better for the planet.

Responsible packaging

Every order ships plastic-free in recyclable boxes with compostable peanuts and soy-based inks.

Climate neutral

We measure, reduce, and offset 100% of our carbon footprint in collaboration with certified partners.
